Our services

MyBooker

MyBooker is a personal appointment scheduling system that allows users to generate a booking link for flexible scheduling of appointments and events. Potential clients can easily book appointments through this link.

  • Increased Booking Rates
    Streamlined booking process helps boost appointment rates and client satisfaction.
  • Automated Reminders
    Automatic email reminders reduce no-shows and missed appointments.
  • Easy Integration
    Easily integrates with existing websites or social media platforms for enhanced user experience and operational efficiency.

NeverBanned

NeverBanned monitors Stripe transaction records to detect and notify users of potential fraudulent or anomalous transactions in real-time, preventing account freezes or dispute fees.

  • Customizable Rules
    Users can tailor detection rules to match their specific transaction monitoring needs.
  • Risk Mitigation
    Reduces financial losses and operational disruptions caused by fraudulent activities or disputes.
  • Efficient Dispute Handling
    Automates dispute resolution processes to streamline refunds and resolve issues promptly.
